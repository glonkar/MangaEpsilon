What?

MangaEpsilon is the ultimate way to read, view and store your favorite manga. Powered by MangaEden, M.E. aims to provide a sleak Modern (also known as Metro) UI for easy of use, without sacaficing the power of a desktop application.

How?

To achieve such a level of minimalism, M.E. utilizes a GUI (Graphical User Interface) toolkit called MahApps.Metro (MA.M for short) to emulate the Windows 8 Metro look and feel. However, you shouldn't assume that just because it looks Metro, it lacks the capability of a regular Windows application. M.E. retains many of the standard Windows 'norms' such as a Context Menu when you right click.

Open Data

M.E. utilizes JSON (Javascript Object Notation) to store it's data files. In order to keep the read/write times down, we use the Json.NET library, better known as Newtonsoft.Json. Because we use JSON, it is possible to hand modify the data fixes should a corruption occur.

Reduced Startup Time

In order to reduce startup time after the initial run, we cache the entire MangaEden on your computer. Don't worry, it is less than a few MBs (Megabytes). Covers are also cached in order to cut down on network usage and to enable offline-usage.
