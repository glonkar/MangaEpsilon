What?

MangaEpsilon is the ultimate way to read, view and store your favorite manga. Powered by MangaEden, M.E. aims to provide a sleek Modern (also known as Metro) UI for easy of use, without sacaficing the power of a desktop application.

How?

To achieve such a level of minimalism, M.E. utilizes a GUI (Graphical User Interface) toolkit called MahApps.Metro (MA.M for short) to emulate the Windows 8 Metro look and feel. However, you shouldn't assume that just because it looks Metro, it lacks the capability of a regular Windows application. M.E. retains many of the standard Windows 'norms' such as a Context Menu when you right click.

Open Data

M.E. utilizes JSON (Javascript Object Notation) to store it's data files. In order to keep the read/write times down, we use the Json.NET library, better known as Newtonsoft.Json. Because we use JSON, it is possible to hand modify the data fixes should a corruption occur.

Reduced Startup Time

In order to reduce startup time after the initial run, we cache the entire MangaEden on your computer. Don't worry, it is less than a few MBs (Megabytes). Covers are also cached in order to cut down on network usage and to enable offline-usage.

Why?

Kind of hard to read your favorite manga offline if its only on a website. Sure, there are dozens of apps to do it on mobile devices (phones, tablets, etc) but what about your desktop? MangaEpsilon fills in the gap by allowing you, the user, to ability to download your favorite manga and have them for offline usage.

Features

Sleek, Modern User Interface

No more hunting for the manga you want. It's all right there.

You can change the colors to one of the select styles to better suit your taste.

Informative

Found a manga that you think you may like? MangaEpsilon will give you the run-down:

  1. Gives you a summary of the Manga.
  2. Shows you what others thing of the Manga on MyAnimeList.
  3. Displays possibly related Manga to you, such as prequels.
M.E. would also give you the ability to view chapters of that manga (provided that it exist on MangaEden).

An added bonus? M.E. comes with a built-in notifications system. That way, you'll know when your downloads have completed!

A Ninja

M.E. has the option to 'minimize to tray' when you minimize the main window in order to free up some space in your taskbar.

A Mailman

Once you add a manga to your 'favorites', M.E. will automatically download the latest chapter if it detects a new chapter while it is running.

More features coming soon!
